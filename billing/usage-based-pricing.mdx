With usage-based pricing, you pay based on how much you use, no monthly subscriptions and monthly minimums. As you scale, you get up to 50% discount on per credit.

## Volume discounts

Based on how many credits you use per month, you get a discount on the credits on that range. See the table below for details on the per credit cost for each range.

<Tabs>

| 0 - 100,000             | 0%                                | $0.900                |

| 1,000,001 - 5,000,000   | 20%                               | $0.720                |
| 5,000,001 - 10,000,000  | 30%                               | $0.630                |

You can use our [calculator](https://magichour.ai/api#api-cost-calculator) to estimate your total cost based on your estimated usage. Volume discount is factored into the calculation.
