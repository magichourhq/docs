## Language Support

The Auto Subtitle Generator uses **Whisper AI** for speech recognition and subtitle generation, providing industry-leading accuracy and language support.

### Automatic Language Detection

- **No manual language selection required** - Whisper automatically detects the spoken language in your video
- **High accuracy detection** - Trained on multilingual data to reliably identify 99+ languages
- **Mixed language support** - Can handle videos with multiple languages spoken

## Output Format

- **MP4 video with embedded subtitles** - Your original video with subtitles burned into the video
- **Properly formatted captions** optimized for readability and visual appeal
- **Speaker detection** when multiple speakers are present
- **Professional styling** with customizable subtitle appearance

## Best Practices

- **Audio quality**: Higher quality audio produces more accurate subtitles
- **Clear speech**: Videos with clear, unobstructed speech work best
- **Background noise**: Minimize background music and noise for optimal results
- **Video length**: Works with videos from a few seconds to several hours
